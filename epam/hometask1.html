MIs young spinners Kumar Kartikeya

posted on 29 june, 2022 (sports)

Australian legendary Chinaman bowler Bradd Hogg was seen imparting his knowledge on spin bowling to the young Mumbai Indian’s spinners Kumar Kartikeya and Raghav Goyal during a practice session in the ongoing Indian Premiere League season.“He is one of my favourite bowlers and I admired him since my childhood. He is a very positive person. Like…. he used to tell me, how he fights every situation and he told me a few things about how to stay positive every time and how to pick up yourself and how to push yourself. So I learnt a lot from him,” said young left-arm orthodox spinner Raghav Goyal.


India records 4,435 fresh COVID cases

posted on 9 July, 2022 (Health)

Apart from the respiratory system, COVID-19 can also impact your heart. Responding to several recent deaths among youngsters in connection with heart attacks, health minister Mansukh Mandaviya told NDTV, "We saw so many young artistes, athletes, sportspersons...they died on stage while performing. We all saw that, and reports started coming in from several places. We needed to investigate."As the COVID-19 pandemic continues to wreak havoc worldwide, researchers and healthcare professionals are still trying to understand the long-term effects of the disease on patients.


ChatGPT and AI are transforming education

posted on 12 August, 2022 (Education)

The transformation of education by ChatGPT and AI can be seen in various ways. These technologies are bringing in innovative changes to the traditional education system, making it more engaging, personalised, and accessible to students all over the world. Artificial Intelligence has been around for decades, but its impact on education has been limited due to the lack of accessibility and affordability. However, in the last 4 months, access to AI and ChatGPT has become more widespread and affordable, creating a huge wave in education.
